India Today vs Outlook
Q1 2025 Analysis
Why Outlook steals volume
Heavy reliance on multi-page Govt booklets and clustered Education "Gamechanger" features compared to India Today's dispersed setup.
Key Gaps vs India Today
🔴 Threat
🏛️
Massive Government Booklets
Insight: Outlook secured a 28-page booklet insert from Rajasthan Tourism and a 16-page DSC initiative from Assam Govt.

India Today Gap: We get 1-2 pagers from UP/Goa/Uttarakhand, but miss these mega-insert booklets. Propose "IT Custom Mini-Mags" to govts instead of single pages.
🟠 Missed Opportunity
🎓
Bundled Education Features
Insight: Outlook runs specific blocks like "FEATURE GAMECHANGERS IN EDUCATION", scooping up regional universities (Xavier, Shiv Nadar, GLA, UEM).

IT Strategy: While IT gets standalone ads (Sathyabama, Galgotia), grouping Tier-2/3 institutes into a high-credibility "IT Education Special" can upsell them to double-page spreads.
